    Spa Pedicure

    You arrive with bare nails. Dry cuticle work done with an e-file, along with some gentle callus reduction. Nail shaping, and massage. The polish of your choice is applied.

    ~50 min

    Spa Natural Manicure

    You arrive with bare nails. Dry cuticle work and gentle callus reduction is done with an e-file. Deeper sole exfoliation done with a scrub and e-file, and deep hydration is added with lotion, an e-file, and oil.

    ~50 min

    Foot Care

    You arrive with bare nails. After a consulation, this service consists of nail trimming, foot inspection, and gentle callus reduction by a registered nurse. Feet will then be rehydrated with lotion. This service is excellent for those struggling to care for their feet, as well as diabetics, seniors, and immunocompromised clients.

    ~30 min


    Foot Care + Pedicure

    You arrive with bare nails. Foot care will be completed, followed by cuticle work, rehydration with lotion, and the polish of your choice.

    ~60 min

    Product Removal (add-on)

    For your safety and mine, I will only remove product that I have previously applied. Please specify at time of booking whether it is for regular or gel polish removal.

    ~5 min for regular or semi-gel polish

    ~20 minutes for gel polish

    Deep Exfoliation and Hydration (add-on)

    An e-file will be used to gently exfoliate the foot, lotion and oil will be applied to deeply hydrate the skin.

    Onyfix Ingrown Toenail Treatment (add-on)

    This is a painless and non-surgical option to treat ingrown or involuted toenails. It works by applying a hard composite to the nail which reshapes it as it grows out. Multiple applications over time are required. Safe for children, seniors, and even diabetics. Cost is per strip.

    Mobile Pedicure/Foot Care

    Either Foot care or a spa pedicure. Please specify which service at time of booking. Please reach out to me prior to booking to make sure your area (Madison area) is included, as all travel time is included in cost.
