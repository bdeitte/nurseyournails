    • About Us

    Nurse Your Nails, located in Middleton, Wisconsin, provides specialized nail and foot care services to the greater Madison area. As a registered nurse who transitioned from acute care, I created this practice to meet the need for manicure and foot care services performed with true hospital-grade sterilization and clinical expertise. My focus is on delivering a relaxing, rejuvenating experience that prioritizes your safety, comfort, and well-being. Both in-studio and mobile services are offered, ensuring accessibility even for those who require specialized support or prefer a non-traditional salon environment. As well as traditional self-care seekers, my services cater to individuals with diabetes, chronic health conditions, cancer, or those recovering from surgery, as well as clients receiving hospice care seeking compassionate care.
