Manicures

Spa Manicure

You arrive with bare nails. Spa style manicure and hand massage. I use an e-file and provide dry cuticle work, nail shaping, and the polish of your choice.

~ 45 min

Spa Natural Manicure

You arrive with bare nails. Spa style manicure and hand massage. Dry cuticle work with an e-file, nail shaping, and deep exfoliation and hydration done with an e-file. No polish applied.

~ 35 min

Structured Gel Manicure

You arrive with bare nails. This is a spa style manicure with dry cuticle work with an e-file, hand massage, nail shaping, and either a BIAB or hard gel from the Light Elegance line for a structured manicure. Which one chosen will be decided during consultation. Includes the polish of your choice.

~90 min


Deep Exfoliation and Hydration (add-on)

This is an add-on service where I use an e-file to deeply and safely exfoliate your hands, as well as an exfoliating scrub. Deep hydration is done with oils and the e-file. Followed by lotion. Perfect add-on in the winter! Your hands feel baby smooth for days upon completion.

~10 min

Product Removal (add-on)

For my safety and yours, I will only remove product that I put on. If you do need product removed during your service, please select this add-on.

~5 min for regular or semi-gel polish

~20 min for gel

Extensions (add-on)

I will use a Light Elegance BIAB or hard gel to sculpt a natural looking extension to your nail. This is perfect for when a nail breaks, or you have a special occasion coming up and want a little extra length. Charge is per nail.

~2 min per nail

Minimal Nail Art (add-on)

I can create minimal nail art. Examples would be french tips on all nails, or, art on a total of no more than 4 fingers. Please add-on service at time of booking, so I can make sure we have enough time allotted to complete the service. I encourage you to send me inspiration pictures via this site when booking.

~15 min


More Nail Art (add-on)

I can create even more nail art, up to 8 fingers. If you only want up to 4, please book Minimal Nail Art instead.

~25 min

Mobile Manicure

Your nails are bare. Spa manicure done at the location of your choice (hospital, other healthcare facility, or home.) Dry cuticle work done with an e-file, nail shaping, massage, and the polish of your choice applied. This is for the Madison area, please contact me to ask if location is in the area I'm able to travel to. All travel time is included in cost.

~45 min

Structured Gel Rebalance

You arrive without removing product. Color is removed using an e-file, and gel structure is reduced. A dry manicure then takes place. New BIAB or hard gel applied to rebalance the apex appropriately to keep nail strength. If you would like to add a colored polish, please book the Structured Gel Manicure instead.

~65 min

Full Set of Extensions or Soft Gel Tips (Gel X)

You arrive with bare nails. After a consultation either soft gel tips or extensions will be applied to the nails. This will part of a full spa manicure, and will include the polish application of your choice and minimal nail art. 

~120 min

Silent Service ($0 add-on)

This is a no cost service that can be chosen at time of booking or upon arrival. Getting your nails done should be a pampering service that provides comfort and meets your needs. Sometimes you need to just put on earbuds and enjoy a show while getting your nails done, and that is OK. By selecting this add-on, you are alerting me to your preference for quiet and no small talk.
