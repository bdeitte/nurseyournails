    Welcome! If you’ve been searching for a place where you can receive nail and foot care in a safe, comforting environment from a highly trained professional, you’ve come to the right spot. I’m here to meet you exactly where you are in your journey and help your nails and feet look—and feel—their very best.

    Nurse Your Nails

    Why Choose Me?

    Your nail and foot health comes first. With advanced sanitation, careful technique, and a soothing environment, I deliver beautiful, natural results you can trust—every single visit.



    Hospital Grade Sanitation

    As a nurse, I understand the critical importance of proper sterilization and infection control. All tools are sterilized using a medical-grade autoclave, and I exclusively use EPA-registered disinfectants on every surface. To further ensure a clean, healthy environment, my studio is equipped with professional dust extractors and air purifiers to protect both your health and mine.

    Soothing Environment

    I’ve designed my studio to be as inviting and relaxing as it is clean. My goal is to eliminate the common stresses and distractions of a busy nail salon and provide you with a tranquil, comfortable environment where you can truly unwind. I’m also proud to offer the only Silent Service option in Madison, giving you a peaceful experience tailored to your needs.

    Natural Nail and Gel Nail Specialist

    My advanced training emphasizes natural nail care and whole-body wellness. All products I use are HEMA-free, prioritize nail and foot health, and are safe for many clients with gel allergies. Protecting your natural nails is always my top priority, and after a thorough consultation, I will recommend the products and techniques best suited to your individual needs.

    Advanced Training and Certifications

    I completed training for dry manicuring, and I have also completed an extensive training program through Light Elegance to ensure their products are applied correctly and safely, allowing me to protect my clients’ nail health while achieving exceptional results. I also am certified in Onyfixy application and in using Podoexpert products.

    RN and Foot Care Certification

    I earned my RN and BSN in 2005 and spent more than 15 years working in acute care. I will also be obtaining my Foot Care Certification as a medical provider through the Wound, Ostomy, and Continence Nursing Certification Board in April 2026. This nationally recognized certification is approved by Medicare.
